Why is it important?

Our digital environment is becoming increasingly important, it is no longer gone thinking and our everyday life takes place more and more with or even in it. One more reason thinking about how we want to shape this environment, how we live in it want.

In addition to advantages and opportunities, the Internet also brings disadvantages and Dangers with themselves. Especially the sneaky tricks of many internet companies, mostly Online shops and social media platforms are increasingly contributing to that Internet is falling for many. Because many companies do not shy away from theirs Users: manipulate inside by underhanded methods to gain their own profit increase. The design and structure of the User interface, with all its information and interaction options. Because this are often designed to mislead and deceive the user and ultimately to confuse them induce an action that is not in his interest. These methods are used in also known as Dark UX Patterns. They make use of knowledge from behavioral psychology and use all possible weak points of the Users: such as impatience, ignorance or even gullibility to carry out their intentions.

What’s the idea?

To help this problem a little bit out I thought how about something which can identify these dangerous elements for us, esepcially for people
Important to me was exspacially that... Transparency, no guardianship and subtle Design

How does ist work?

Install and activate

At first install the tool, so that is shown in your Browser Menü. Up to here you can activate and deactivate it at any time over the User Interface.

Your settings your decision

You can decide how much the tool influences your view over the site. You can use the Settings shown in the User Interface but when you want to decide more in detail you can do that over the official setting site, which you can reach over the settings icon.

Surf in freedom

Depending on your settings you will be warned and informed about danger or elements will be hidden so you don’t even notice that there had could been danger. So you can learn much about the different Dark UX Pattern.

Get navigated when lost in Roach Motel

A very special Dark UX Pattern is called Roach Motel. Here it is easy for users to confirm a abo but in the other way around it is very hard to cancel the plan again. The Tool can navigate you to the right places trough Experiences from other users.

Report Dark UX Pattern

Whenever you notice a Dark UX Pattern which is still unknown to the tool you can report it. Just by right Click you can mark the UX Pattern an klick report. In this way new Dark UX Pattern can be found very fast and influenced by us very fast.
